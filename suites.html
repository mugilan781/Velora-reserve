Material & Space

Anatomy of a Sanctuary

Quiet luxury is tactile. Every sanctuary at VÉLORA RESERVE is engineered with a meticulous spatial rhythm, marrying ancient regional building methodologies with high-end acoustic and thermal science to isolate you from the outside world.

Pure Travertine

Sourced from regional quarries, hand-laid raw stone flooring naturally cools the interiors and absorbs echo, preserving silent peace.

Acoustic Teak

Reclaimed local hardwoods frame high-pitched cathedral ceilings, providing soft natural dampening for local rainfall sounds.

Living Greenery

Sanctuary walls are flanked with organic vine structures and low-transpiration air-cleaning plants that cool naturally.

Thermal Mass

Double-layer outer walls built of local volcanic sand elements reflect 92% of ambient sun heat, ensuring absolute cooling mass.
