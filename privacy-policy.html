Privacy Policy

Your trust, privacy, and security remain at the heart of every VÉLORA RESERVE experience.

Last Updated
June 2026
Section A

Information We Collect

At VÉLORA RESERVE, we collect only the information necessary to deliver an extraordinary and personalized luxury hospitality experience. We are committed to transparency in every interaction.

  • Personal Information — Full name, date of birth, nationality, and identification documents required for secure reservation processing and regulatory compliance.
  • Reservation Information — Stay dates, suite preferences, special occasion details, dining requirements, and bespoke experience selections that allow us to craft your perfect sanctuary.
  • Contact Details — Email address, phone number, and postal address used exclusively for booking confirmations, concierge coordination, and pre-arrival curation.
  • Preferences & Interests — Dietary requirements, wellness preferences, pillow selections, preferred fragrances, and activity interests that enable our Butler Alchemists to anticipate your desires.
Section B

How We Use Information

Your information is used with the utmost care and solely to enhance the quality of your VÉLORA RESERVE experience. We never sell, rent, or share your personal data with unauthorized third parties.

  • Reservation Management — Processing bookings, confirming availability, coordinating seaplane and yacht transfers, and managing your complete stay lifecycle from inquiry to checkout.
  • Personalized Experiences — Tailoring in-suite amenities, curating bespoke dining menus, arranging private excursions, and calibrating wellness rituals to your individual preferences.
  • Customer Support — Responding to concierge requests, resolving service inquiries, managing special occasion arrangements, and providing 24/7 VIP guest assistance across all properties.
  • Service Improvements — Analyzing anonymized usage patterns to refine our hospitality standards, enhance our digital platforms, and develop new experiential offerings for our guests.
Section C

Communications

We communicate with our guests through carefully curated channels, ensuring every correspondence reflects the quiet luxury standards of VÉLORA RESERVE.

  • Booking Confirmations — Instant digital confirmations with detailed itineraries, suite specifications, and pre-arrival preparation guides delivered upon reservation completion.
  • Reservation Updates — Timely notifications regarding booking status changes, approval confirmations, villa assignments, and check-in readiness communications.
  • Guest Service Notifications — Personalized alerts for concierge requests, dining reservations, spa appointments, and curated experience schedules throughout your stay.
  • Marketing Preferences — Optional subscription to our curated newsletter featuring seasonal highlights, exclusive villa offerings, and invitation-only retreat announcements. You may opt out at any time.
Section D

Data Protection

Protecting your personal data is a paramount responsibility at VÉLORA RESERVE. We employ enterprise-grade security infrastructure to safeguard every piece of information entrusted to us.

  • Secure Systems — All guest data is stored on hardened, access-controlled servers with multi-layered firewall protections and continuous intrusion detection monitoring.
  • Encryption — Industry-standard TLS/SSL encryption protects all data in transit, while AES-256 encryption secures stored information across our digital platforms.
  • Access Control — Strict role-based access policies ensure that only authorized personnel with legitimate business needs can access guest information, with comprehensive audit trails.
  • Data Security Practices — Regular security assessments, vulnerability scanning, employee training programs, and compliance reviews maintain our commitment to data stewardship excellence.
Section E

Cookies & Analytics

Our digital platforms use minimal, purposeful tracking technologies to ensure a seamless and optimized browsing experience for our guests.

  • Website Optimization — Essential cookies maintain your session preferences, language selections, and browsing continuity to deliver a fluid, uninterrupted luxury digital experience.
  • Analytics Tracking — We utilize anonymized analytics to understand how guests navigate our digital sanctuary, allowing us to refine content, improve user journeys, and enhance booking flows.
  • Performance Monitoring — Technical performance cookies help us identify and resolve loading issues, ensuring our website delivers the same standard of excellence as our physical properties.
Section F

Third-Party Services

To deliver our comprehensive luxury hospitality services, we partner with carefully vetted third-party providers who adhere to equivalent data protection standards.

  • Payment Providers — Secure, PCI-DSS compliant payment processing partners handle all financial transactions. VÉLORA RESERVE never stores complete payment card details on our systems.
  • Reservation Systems — Integrated booking and property management platforms that coordinate availability, guest profiles, and stay logistics across our global portfolio of properties.
  • Communication Platforms — Trusted email delivery, SMS notification, and WhatsApp concierge communication partners that enable seamless, real-time guest coordination worldwide.
Section G

Guest Rights

As a valued guest of VÉLORA RESERVE, you maintain complete authority over your personal information. We respect and facilitate the following rights at all times.

  • Access Information — You may request a comprehensive summary of all personal data we hold about you, including reservation history, preference profiles, and communication records.
  • Update Information — You have the right to correct, amend, or update any personal information at any time through your guest dashboard or by contacting our Privacy Concierge.
  • Request Deletion — You may request the complete erasure of your personal data from our systems, subject to applicable legal retention obligations and active reservation commitments.
  • Marketing Preferences — You can modify your communication preferences, opt out of promotional materials, or unsubscribe from newsletters at any time without affecting your guest experience.
Section H

Contact Regarding Privacy

For any questions, concerns, or requests related to this Privacy Policy or the handling of your personal data, please reach out to our dedicated Privacy Concierge team. We are committed to responding within 48 hours.
